## Functional Requirements

- The system should accept agricultural parameters from users.
- The system should analyze soil and environmental conditions.
- The system should predict the most suitable crop.
- The system should display crop recommendations through a web interface.
- The system should provide accurate and reliable results.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- User-friendly interface.
- Fast response time.
- High prediction accuracy.
- Scalability for future enhancements.
- Secure and reliable operation.

## Stakeholders

- Farmers
- Agricultural Consultants
- Researchers
- Government Agricultural Departments
