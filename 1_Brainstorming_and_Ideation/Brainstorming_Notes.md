## Problem Identification

Farmers often struggle to identify the most suitable crop for cultivation due to changing environmental conditions and varying soil properties.

## Key Challenges

- Unpredictable weather conditions.
- Lack of scientific crop selection methods.
- Poor resource utilization.
- Low agricultural productivity.
- Financial losses due to improper crop selection.

## Proposed Approach

Develop a Machine Learning-based Crop Recommendation System that:

- Accepts agricultural parameters as input.
- Analyzes soil and climate conditions.
- Predicts the most suitable crop.
- Provides recommendations through a user-friendly web application.

## Technologies Considered

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Scikit-Learn
- Flask
- HTML
- CSS

## Expected Outcomes

- Smart crop recommendation.
- Improved decision-making.
- Higher crop yield.
- Enhanced farming efficiency.
- Sustainable agricultural development.
