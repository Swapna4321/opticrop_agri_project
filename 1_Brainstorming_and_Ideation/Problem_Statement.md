# Problem Statement

Agriculture plays a vital role in ensuring food security and supporting the economy. However, farmers often face difficulties in selecting the most suitable crop for cultivation due to variations in soil nutrients, climate conditions, rainfall patterns, and environmental factors.

Traditional crop selection methods mainly rely on experience and assumptions, which may not always produce the best results. Incorrect crop selection can lead to reduced productivity, inefficient resource utilization, and financial losses for farmers.

To address this challenge, the OptiCrop project proposes a Machine Learning-based Crop Recommendation System that analyzes agricultural parameters such as Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), Temperature, Humidity, pH, and Rainfall. Based on these inputs, the system recommends the most suitable crop for cultivation.

The primary objective of this project is to assist farmers in making data-driven decisions, improving crop yield, and promoting sustainable agricultural practices through intelligent crop recommendations.
